About Plarto

Plarto is a graphic design student with a strong passion for creativity and visual storytelling. He is constantly exploring new ideas and techniques, developing his ability to communicate concepts through design. Whether working on digital compositions, layouts, or creative projects, he enjoys the process of turning ideas into visually engaging pieces. He is motivated to keep improving his skills and expanding his knowledge in design, always looking for inspiration in both art and everyday life. Outside of his studies, Plarto has a wide range of interests that reflect his creative and curious personality. He enjoys reading, which allows him to explore different perspectives, ideas, and narratives that often inspire his work. Writing is another passion of his, as it helps him express thoughts and emotions while developing his creativity in a different way. Plarto also values spending time outdoors. He enjoys hiking, where he can disconnect, clear his mind, and appreciate nature. These experiences often influence his creative thinking and help him return to his work with a fresh perspective. In addition, he enjoys baking, finding satisfaction in the process of creating something from scratch and paying attention to detail—skills that also translate into his design work. In his free time, he also likes playing video games, which not only serve as entertainment but also inspire him through their visuals, storytelling, and interactive design.
